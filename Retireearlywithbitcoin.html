👤 Personal Details
🏛️ Social Security
How Social Security helps: Each year you're eligible, your benefit is subtracted from your retirement spending first, so your portfolio only has to cover the remaining gap. That means fewer withdrawals from your investments — and often an earlier possible retirement.
💼 Other Retirement Income
How retirement income helps: Rental or business income continues right through retirement (from the year you retire) and is subtracted from your annual spending, rising with inflation — so your portfolio is tapped for less. A pension works like Social Security but on its own start age and inflation terms. Every dollar of ongoing income is a dollar you don't have to withdraw.

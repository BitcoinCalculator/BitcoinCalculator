PIN Calculator

Enter the full date. The calculator builds the base formula, then subtracts day × 366.

Base PIN = year × 317 + (year mod 100) × 89 + month × 613 + 100000
Final PIN = Base PIN - (day × 366)
PIN Result
Example: if date = April 17, 2026, then the calculator subtracts 17 × 366 from the base result.
